Frequently Asked
Questions.

What file formats do you accept?

I accept almost all standard video formats including MP4, MOV, and high-resolution raw files. You can easily share files via Google Drive, Dropbox, or Frame.io.

How fast is delivery?

For standard short-form videos (Reels/Shorts), delivery is typically 24-48 hours. Larger projects or bulk orders will have a custom timeline agreed upon during our initial discussion.

Do you provide revisions?

Yes. I want you to be 100% satisfied with the final product. Every project includes structured revision rounds to ensure the edit perfectly matches your vision.

Can you create captions?

Absolutely. I specialize in dynamic, animated captions that are optimized for retention and styled to match your unique brand identity.

Can you source B-rolls?

Yes. I have access to premium stock libraries and can source high-quality, relevant B-roll footage to elevate your storytelling.

Do you edit podcasts?

Yes. I can edit full-length podcasts as well as repurpose long-form podcast episodes into multiple high-performing viral clips.

Can you repurpose long-form content?

Yes. I can extract the most engaging moments from your YouTube videos, webinars, or livestreams and turn them into highly engaging short-form content.

Do you work with agencies?

Yes, I frequently partner with marketing and creative agencies on a white-label basis, ensuring consistent quality and strict adherence to deadlines.

Can you match an existing style?

Definitely. If you have an established brand style or a specific editing reference, just share it with me and I will replicate the exact pacing, coloring, and motion design.
